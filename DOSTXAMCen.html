DOST - Advanced Manufacturing Center (AMCen) Ilocos Region

THE CHALLENGE

Traditional manufacturing in the Philippines struggles with costly prototyping, limited access to advanced tools, and slow tech transfer—hindering product development and MSME competitiveness. The AMCen network addresses these gaps by decentralizing advanced manufacturing and offering local 3D printing, prototyping, and digital design services

Services

Additive Manufacturing & Prototyping

We provide 3D printing solutions that turn digital designs into functional prototypes and custom parts, enabling faster innovation and reduced development costs.

Product Development Clinics

Our experts guide innovators and enterprises through consultations to refine product concepts, improve designs, and prepare technologies for market entry.

3D Scanning & Reverse Engineering

Using advanced 3D scanners, we capture precise digital models of existing objects for design replication, modification, and performance enhancement..

Training & Workshops

We offer hands-on training and specialized workshops to build skills in advanced manufacturing, 3D design, and digital fabrication technologies

Collaboration & Referral

We connect clients with research institutions, industry partners, and government agencies to strengthen innovation networks and accelerate project success.

About AMCen

The Advanced Manufacturing Center — I ILocos Region (AMCEN ILocos Region) is the region’s premier hub for additive manufacturing, digital design, and prototyping. Operated under the DOST regional network and linked with DOST–MIRDC technical expertise, AMCEN ILocos Region supports innovators, startups, MSMEs, students, and government programs by providing access to modern 3D printing systems, CAD resources, and hands-on technical assistance. We bridge R&D and production so regional ideas become competitive products.
